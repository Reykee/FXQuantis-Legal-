1. Introduction

FXQuantis is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the FXQuantis mobile application ("App") — an AI-powered forex analytics platform that delivers real-time market intelligence, AI trading signals, whale-order alerts, and copy-trading insights.

By using FXQuantis, you consent to this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Full name and email address (for account creation)
  • Password (stored as a secure hash — never in plain text)
  • Broker API credentials — OANDA API key & account ID, or MetaApi token & account ID (stored encrypted, used solely to fetch your live account data)
  • Subscription and payment information (processed entirely by the App Store or Google Play via RevenueCat — we never store card details)
  • Feedback and support messages

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • Device identifiers (IDFV, advertising ID where permitted)
  • IP address and approximate geographic region
  • App usage patterns and session data
  • Crash reports and performance diagnostics
  • Expo push notification token (for delivering real-time alerts)

2.3 Broker & Trading Account Data

  • Account balance, equity, and unrealised P&L (fetched live from your connected broker)
  • Open positions and trade history (used to render your Dashboard equity curve)
  • Margin utilisation and risk metrics

This data is fetched in real time from your broker via their official API and is never permanently stored beyond the current session cache. Disconnecting your broker immediately revokes our access.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To create, verify, and manage your account
  • To render your live Dashboard, equity curve, and portfolio analytics
  • To deliver AI-generated forex signals, whale alerts, and copy-trading insights
  • To send push notifications for price alerts and signal changes (with your consent)
  • To manage your subscription entitlements (Standard, Pro, Institutional)
  • To detect fraud, abuse, or security threats
  • To improve the accuracy of AI models and analytics (using anonymised, aggregated data only)
  • To respond to support requests
We do NOT sell your personal data or broker credentials to third parties.

4. Sharing of Information

We may share your information with:

  • OANDA / MetaApi — to authenticate and retrieve your live account data on your behalf
  • RevenueCat — to manage in-app subscription purchases and entitlements
  • Apple App Store / Google Play — payment processing for subscriptions
  • Expo / Firebase Cloud Messaging — to deliver push notifications to your device
  • Analytics providers — anonymised, aggregated usage data only (no personal identifiers)
  • Legal authorities — if required by a valid court order or applicable law

5. Data Storage & Security

We store your data securely using industry-standard encryption (AES-256 at rest, TLS 1.3 in transit). Broker API credentials are stored with additional envelope encryption and are never logged or exposed in plaintext. We retain account data for as long as your account is active. Upon account deletion, personal data is removed within 30 days, except where retention is required by applicable law.

6. Your Rights

  • Access your data — Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correct your data — Update inaccurate information via Profile → Personal Information.
  • Delete your account — In the app: Profile → Settings → Account → Delete Account. By email: support@fxquantis.com
  • Disconnect your broker — Remove all broker credentials and cached account data at any time via Profile → Connected Broker → Disconnect.
  • Withdraw notification consent — Opt out of push notifications at any time via Settings → Notifications or your device's system settings.
  • Data portability — Request an export of your account data in a machine-readable format.

Contact us at support@fxquantis.com for any data requests.

7. Children's Privacy

FXQuantis is strictly intended for users who are 18 years of age or older. Forex trading involves significant financial risk and is not appropriate for minors. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If we discover that a minor has registered, we will immediately close the account and delete all associated data.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes via in-app notification or email. Continued use of the App after the effective date constitutes acceptance.
